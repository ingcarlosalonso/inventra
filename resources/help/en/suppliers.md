# Suppliers

The Suppliers module manages data for the companies or individuals that supply products to your business. Suppliers are used when registering merchandise receipts (stock entries).

## Supplier Attributes

- **Company name**: legal name of the company or person (e.g. "Rodriguez Distribution Co.").
- **Tax ID**: tax identification number.
- **Contact name**: contact person within the supplier.
- **Phone**: contact phone number.
- **Email**: email for communication.
- **Address**: fiscal or commercial address.
- **Notes**: internal observations (payment terms, delivery times, etc.).

## Creating a Supplier

1. Click **New Supplier**.
2. Fill in the company name (required).
3. Enter the tax ID, contact name, phone, email, and address (all optional).
4. Add internal notes if needed.
5. Click **Save**.

## Use in Other Modules

Suppliers are used in the **Receptions** (Stock Entry) module. When registering incoming merchandise, you select the supplier from the list.

The **Purchases** report shows reception history by supplier, allowing cost analysis by supplier and period.

## Tips

> **Tip**: Fill in the notes field with the agreed payment terms for each supplier (e.g. "30 days", "cash with 5% discount") to have that information handy when registering receptions.
