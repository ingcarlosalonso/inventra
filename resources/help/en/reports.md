# Reports

The Reports module offers detailed analysis of all business areas: sales, products, clients, stock, cash, and orders. All reports include date filters and Excel export.

## Available Reports

### Sales Report

Analyzes sales performance in the selected period.

**Key metrics:**
- Total period revenue
- Total collected amount
- Total discounts applied
- Average ticket per sale
- Number of sales

**Visualizations:**
- Revenue evolution chart (timeline)
- Complete sales table with filters by client, state, and point of sale

**Filters:** date range, client, point of sale, sale state.

---

### Products Report

Identifies best-selling products and catalog performance.

**Key metrics:**
- Units sold per product
- Revenue generated per product
- Top products ranking

**Visualizations:**
- Bar chart: top products by units
- Bar chart: top products by revenue
- Detailed table by product/presentation

**Filters:** date range, product type.

---

### Payments Report

Analyzes how clients pay and the collection flow.

**Key metrics:**
- Total collected per payment method
- Daily/weekly/monthly collections
- Average daily collections

**Visualizations:**
- Daily collections chart (timeline)
- Pie chart: distribution by payment method
- Movements table with method, amount, and date

**Filters:** date range, payment method, point of sale.

---

### Inventory Report

Shows the current stock status of all products.

**Key metrics:**
- Total products in stock
- Products with low stock (below minimum)
- Products with no stock

**Stock table:**
- Product and presentation
- Current stock and minimum stock
- Stock status: OK / Low / Out of stock (with colors)
- Stock value (quantity x price)

**Filters:** product type, stock status, name search.

---

### Daily Cashes Report

Financial summary of all cash registers in the period.

**Metrics:**
- Total income per point of sale
- Total expenses
- Cash differences (if any)
- Net balance for the period

**Table:**
- Detail per cash: opening, closing, income, expenses, difference.

**Filters:** date range, point of sale, status (open/closed).

---

### Orders Report

Analysis of delivery performance and logistics.

**Metrics:**
- Orders by state
- Orders by courier
- Average delivery time
- Total orders and amount

**Visualizations:**
- Order distribution by state
- Courier ranking by delivery count

**Filters:** date range, state, courier, point of sale.

---

### Clients Report

Identifies most active clients and their purchasing behavior.

**Metrics:**
- Top clients by amount purchased
- Number of purchases per client
- Average ticket per client

**Filters:** date range, client.

---

### Purchases Report

Analyzes supplier purchases (receptions) in the period.

**Metrics:**
- Total invested in purchases
- Purchases per supplier
- Average cost per reception

**Table:**
- Detail of each reception with supplier, date, items, and total.

**Filters:** date range, supplier.

---

## Exporting to Excel

All reports have an **Export Excel** button that downloads an XLSX file with the report table data.

## Tips

> **Tip**: Check the Inventory report weekly to detect low-stock products before they run out.

> **Tip**: The Payments report shows which payment methods your clients prefer, useful for negotiating terms with card providers.

> **Tip**: Export reports to Excel to create your own dashboards or share data with your accountant.
