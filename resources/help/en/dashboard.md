# Dashboard

The Dashboard is Inventra's main screen. It provides a consolidated view of your business's current status: sales, cash, orders, stock, and more.

## Main KPIs

When you log in, you'll see a row of cards showing the following day and month indicators:

- **Today's revenue**: total amount invoiced in sales today.
- **Today's sales**: number of sales registered today.
- **Collected today**: total amount actually collected (sum of payments registered today).
- **Month revenue**: total invoiced in the current month.
- **Month sales**: number of sales in the current month.
- **Active orders**: orders in non-final states (in preparation, on the way, etc.).
- **Pending quotes**: quotes not yet converted to a sale or order.
- **Total clients**: clients registered in the system.
- **Low-stock products**: products whose current stock is below the configured minimum.
- **Open cash registers**: points of sale with a currently open daily cash register.

## Charts

The Dashboard includes several charts for trend analysis:

- **Sales evolution (30 days)**: timeline with the daily sales amount over the last 30 days. Helps identify peaks and drops in activity.
- **Payment methods**: chart showing the percentage of each payment method used in sales during the period.
- **Orders by state**: distribution of active orders by state (Pending, On the way, Delivered, etc.).
- **Weekly sales comparison**: compares current week's sales against the previous week.
- **Top 5 products**: the five best-selling products by units or revenue.

## Quick Lists

Below the charts you'll find:

- **Open cash registers**: list of points of sale with an open cash register, showing the current balance of each.
- **Recent sales**: the latest registered sales with client, amount, and state.
- **Low-stock products**: table of products below the minimum stock threshold, with current quantity and minimum configured.

## Quick Actions

In the system's top bar you'll find quick access buttons for the most frequent operations:

- **New Sale**: opens the sale creation form directly.
- **New Order**: opens the order creation form.
- **Quotes**: navigates to the quotes list.
- **Reports**: navigates to the advanced reports module.

## Refreshing Data

The Dashboard loads data on entry. You can refresh data using the **Refresh** button (reload icon) in the top right corner of the main panel.

## Tips

> **Tip**: Check the low-stock panel regularly to anticipate restocking before running out of product.

> **Tip**: The payment methods chart helps negotiate better commissions with card providers or identify customer preferences.
